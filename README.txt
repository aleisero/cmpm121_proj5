Controls: WASD or arrow keys to move
Rotate the camera: Q and E
P to play from title screen, and to return to title screen (pause)
Space to jump

3D assets used: https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/characters/humanoids/character-pack-free-sample-79870

concrete texture: https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/2d/textures-materials/concrete/concrete-materials-lite-materials-collection-70619
